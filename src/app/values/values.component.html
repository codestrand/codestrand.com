Our values

Laser focus

Working on a single project at a time, no-one assigned to a project will be interrupted by context changes and balancing tasks.

Reliable

Our technology stack is based on wide-spread, up-to-date Open Source projects, best practices and patterns. This ensures our work will be interoperable with most projects. It also makes bringing extra help or maintaining the projects inside your organisation will be easier and faster than custom, obscure or out-of-date frameworks and technologies.

Fair

We firmly believe in equality and we will fight for those who have not had a fair chance. We want to train, hire and promote the next generation of developers.

Contributing

codestrand was born out of collaborating on community events. because of this we're committed to support our local community by sponsoring events and donating paid time so our team members can participate by organising events, speaking at conferences and meetups, and engaging with the community.

We're determined to share a percentage of our project earnings with Open Source projects that power our work without which our work would just not be possible.
